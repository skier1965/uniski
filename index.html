Freelance cloud consultant: This refers to an independent IT professional who specializes in managing and supporting cloud infrastructure for various clients. They possess expertise in cloud platforms like Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, etc. Their core tasks include designing, migrating, monitoring, and troubleshooting cloud systems for different companies. The "freelance" aspect means they work independently, contracting their services to different clients on a project-by-project basis, rather than being employed by a single company.
